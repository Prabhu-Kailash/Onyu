Skincare Tips

How frequently can I use sheet face mask?

+

You can use sheet masks 1 to 3 times a week, depending on your skin type and the mask's ingredients. If your skin is sensitive or prone to irritation, starting with once a week is a good idea. Always follow up with moisturizer for best results!

Should I do patch test before using face sheet mask?

+

Yes, it's a good idea to do a patch test before using a new sheet mask. Apply a small amount of the product on a discreet area of skin, like your wrist or behind your ear, and wait 24 hours to see if any irritation or allergic reaction occurs. This can help prevent potential skin issues, especially if you have sensitive skin.

How do I choose the right face mask for my skin type?

+

If you have oily skin, look for masks with clay or charcoal to absorb excess oil. For dry skin, hydrating masks with ingredients like hyaluronic acid or glycerin are ideal. Sensitive skin benefits from masks with soothing ingredients like aloe vera.

What ingredients should I avoid in my skincare products?

+

Avoid harsh ingredients like alcohol, sulfates, and artificial fragrances as they can irritate the skin. Parabens and phthalates are also considered harmful by many skincare experts.

How can I reduce dark circles under my eyes?

+

To reduce dark circles, use products with caffeine, vitamin C, or hyaluronic acid. Applying a cold compress or getting enough sleep can also help improve the appearance of under-eye darkness.

How often should I exfoliate my skin?

+

Exfoliate your skin 1-3 times a week, depending on your skin type. Oily or acne-prone skin may benefit from more frequent exfoliation, while sensitive or dry skin should stick to once a week to avoid irritation.

Can I use the same skincare products year-round?

+

It's recommended to adjust your skincare routine based on the season. During colder months, switch to more hydrating products, while lighter, oil-free products are better for summer to prevent clogged pores.
